Plays one `.ogg` at random when the Evaluation screen shows a score with "69"
in a notable result value -- the Simply Love "Nice" easter egg.

This folder ships with `nice.ogg` imported from Simply Love (GPLv3), so the
sound is active out of the box. Drop additional `.ogg` files in to randomize
the pick.

Files starting with `_` are ignored.

Enable / disable the clip with `MachineNiceSound` under `[Theme]` in
deadsync.ini, or from Options > Machine Options > Nice Sound.
