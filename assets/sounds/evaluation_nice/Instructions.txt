Plays one `.ogg` at random when the Evaluation screen shows a score whose
percentage contains "69" anywhere (e.g. 69.42% or 85.69%) -- the Simply Love
"Nice" easter egg.

This folder ships with `nice.ogg` imported from Simply Love (GPLv3), so the
sound is active out of the box. Drop additional `.ogg` files in to randomize
the pick, or remove `nice.ogg` to silence it.

Files starting with `_` are ignored.

Enable / disable the whole feature with `CustomSoundsEnabled` in options.ini.
